Lasagna

Home

Description

Lasagna is made for those occasion where you want to show off your cooking skills to your guests.

It's basically made with the combination of various seasonings and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese Slices
  • Seasonings
  • Butter
  • Green onion
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Take a bowl and crack 2-3 eggs in it.
  2. Add some seasonings and green onions and give it a whisk.
  3. Dip your bread slice in it and place it over your stove on a medium heated pan.
  4. Put some butter on the pan , after cooking your bread , place a cheese slice over a bread and cover it with another.
  5. Your lasagna is ready.
